Lemon Bars

Ingredients

  • Crust
    • 2 cups flour
    • 1 cup butter or margarine
    • 1/2 cup of powdered sugar
  • Lemon Filling
    • 4 eggs
    • 5 - 7 tablespoon of lemon juice
    • 2 cups sugar
    • 4 tablespoons flour
    • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Kitchen Equipment

  • Oven
  • 9" x 12" pan
  • Measuring cups
  • Measuring spoons
  • Hand mixer

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (180 degrees C).
  2. Cream and press crust ingredients into ungreased 9" x 12" pan.
  3. Place pan into oven and bake for 20 minutes.
  4. Mix lemon filling ingredients together until smooth.
  5. Pour lemon filling ontop of baked crust.
  6. Place pan back into the oven and bake for 25 minutes at 350 degrees F.
  7. Sprinkle powdered sugar on top while still warm.
  8. Let cool before cutting into bars.

Tips and Notes

  • To cream means to mix the ingredients until they are smooth.
  • Sprinkling powdered surgar while the bars are warm will help the sugar stick to the bar.
  • I personally do less than a cup of sugar that the recipe calls for as the recipe is more on the sweet side rather than the tart side!
